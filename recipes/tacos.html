Tacos

Description

Tacos are a vibrant, flavorful classic that brings excitement to any meal. This simple recipe pairs seasoned ground beef with warm corn tortillas or crunchy shells. Finished with fresh lime and cilantro, every bite delivers a perfect balance of savory and bright flavors.

The best part about making tacos at home is how customizable they are. Setting up a toppings bar with cheese, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream turns a quick weeknight dinner into a fun, hands-on meal that everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 package taco shells (soft or hard)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Brown meat: Cook ground beef over medium heat until fully browned, then drain any excess fat.
  2. Season: Stir in the taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions; simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm shells: Heat your taco shells or tortillas in a skillet or oven until warm and pliable.
  4. Assemble: Fill each shell with seasoned beef and layer on your favorite toppings like cheese, lettuce, and sour cream.
