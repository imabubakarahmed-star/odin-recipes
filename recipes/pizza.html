Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza is a timeless comfort food that brings the pizzeria experience right into your kitchen. This classic recipe features a crispy, golden crust, a savory tomato sauce, and a generous layer of melty mozzarella cheese. Baked at a high temperature, it achieves a perfectly blistered edge while keeping the inside soft and chewy.

The best part of making pizza at home is how fun and customizable it is. Whether you keep it simple with pepperoni or pile it high with your favorite toppings, it makes for a fantastic weekend cooking project or a fun dinner to share with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Heat oven to 450 F (230 C.)
  2. Shape Roll or stretch dough into a circle.
  3. Assemble Spread sauce, add cheese, and layer pepperoni.
  4. Bake Brush crust with olive oil. Bake 12-15 minutes until golden
